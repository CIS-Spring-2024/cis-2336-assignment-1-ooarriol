Who Am I?

Hi, I'm Oscar, the proud owner of Cougar Den. I'm a current student at UH graduating in 2025 with a CIS major. I love everything that involves food and I love bringing happiness to my customers through food and good customer service. For many years I've wanted to open a place here in school due to the lack of food spots in Sugarland. My culinary passion led me to open such an amazing place here in Sugarland. I hope I'm able to attract many more students into my humble business and that they have a great experience.
